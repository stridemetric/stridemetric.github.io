⚡ Quick start
  • 1. Import your routeClick ⊕ GPX or drag your file. Distance, elevation and profile load automatically.
  • 2. Choose your activity🥾 Hiking · 🏃 Running · 🚴 Cycling
  • 3. Set your profileEnter weight, age, gender and heart rate. FTP optional for cycling.
  • 4. Check your zonesCardio Z1→Z5, power/VAM. Stay in the zone suited to your effort.
  • 5. Analyse your outingSpeed, elevation, power, HR, energy — all from your GPX.
  • 6. Optimize your effortZone, duration or max calorie mode. Click ▶ OPTIMIZE.
🎯 Once your GPX is loaded, Stridely guides you to adapt your pace and nutrition to your route.
